Campus Facilities Every Student Dreams Of

* Smart classrooms across all departments
LCD projectors, digital boards, and internet connected learning environments in every classroom. Not added for accreditation visits used every day.

* Dedicated labs for every programme
CSE and IT have separate computing labs with current software stacks. ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil, Biomedical, Pharmacy and Architecture each have department specific practical labs. Nursing students train at National Hospital, Kozhikode from year one.

* Al and emerging tech integrated into curriculum
CSE and IT programmes include machine learning, data science and Al electives aligned with KTU 2024 curriculum updates. TinkerHub and IEDC run regular Al and full stack workshops open to all branches.

* Faculty with active industry and research experience
Departments are staffed by faculty with postgraduate qualifications. Several faculty members are PhD holders or pursuing research. Guest lectures from industry professionals are part of the academic calendar, not a one off event.

* Both KEAM and management quota seats available
Government allotment through CEE Kerala and direct management quota admissions are open for all B.Tech, Nursing, Pharmacy and Architecture programmes. One form, one call we confirm availability the same day.
